About Us

Stage Fright is anything but afraid to take the spotlight. Blending raw energy with haunting melodies, we’re an up-and-coming band that thrives on the edge of rock and alternative soundscapes. Our music tells stories of late-night anxieties, electric dreams, and the thrill of the unknown. Whether we’re shaking the stage or pouring our hearts into a quiet melody, we bring every note to life.

Join us as we turn fear into fire—one song at a time.
